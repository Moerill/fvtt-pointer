Pointer v2 - Important!

The Pointer module finally got a shiny new configurable settings menu! You can now create your own ping and pointer designs, set your own custom hotkeys (on a per user basis) and even have different designs on a per player basis! So make sure your players go and set their preferred settings as well!
To celebrate v2 the Pointer module got renamed to: PnP - Pings and Pointer!
